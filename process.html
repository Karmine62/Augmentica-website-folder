Seamless Onboarding Solutions

Experience the ease of our onboarding process, where every step is crafted for your convenience. Our seamless onboarding solutions ensure a smooth transition, empowering you to harness the full potential of our servi ces with confidence and clarity

Step-by-Step Onboarding Process

Initial Consultation


Our client onboarding process at Augmentica starts with an initial consultation aimed at comprehending your business objectives, website performance, and market position to strategize effectively. After thorough requirement gathering, including essential content like the number of 3D models that are needed and design preferences, tailored service packages are offered based on your specific needs, budget, and project complexity. Once the package is selected, we initiate the contractual agreement with a 50% downpayment, followed by 25% upon project completion, and the remaining 25% after revisions. This structured approach ensures clarity, customization, and commitment to your project's success.


Project Planning


During project planning, we construct precise timelines that encapsulate all phases and milestones to ensure efficient management and on-time delivery. Resources, tools, and technology are meticulously allocated to optimize team performance for achieving project objectives. Our process involves comprehensive discussions with the client, understanding their vision, and leveraging innovative design tools to create visually captivating and engaging experiences.


Wireframing, Prototyping, and Web Design Process


During the Wireframing and Prototyping phase, we create detailed models for clients to visualize and approve design concepts before development. Active client engagement allows us to gather feedback and make revisions to ensure the design aligns seamlessly with their expectations. In the Web Design Process, we use cutting-edge technologies to transform 2D websites into immersive 3D experiences, incorporating AI tools, virtual product try-ons, and interactive features to enhance user engagement. Our approach involves close collaboration and tailored code implementation to meet the unique needs of clients' e-commerce platforms.


Quality Assurance and Testing


Our Quality Assurance and Testing phase is the backbone for a seamless website launch. This process involves rigorous Quality Assurance testing, meticulously scanning for any glitches, inconsistencies, or security concerns to maintain high standards and address potential vulnerabilities. After this, we carry out live testing to mimic real-time traffic and user engagement, ensuring the site's robustness.


Post-Launch Support


Following the site launch, our commitment doesn't stop. We ensure round-the-clock general support, addressing issues or inquiries within 3-4 days, catering to various complexities. The ongoing process involves regular updates and enhancements, maintaining website relevancy and optimal performance. Our proactive approach involves consistent monitoring of website performance, user behavior, and industry trends to recommend and implement relevant improvements.
